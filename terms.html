The weird things about t-shirts


1-3% Margin of Error
Humans are involved in making your t-shirts so sometimes odd things happen. If mis-prints, or damages do slip through quality control, we will refund you for those.

Re-printing shortages
Due to the nature of setups, projects will not replace shortages that are inside the margin of error. Please notify us if you are ordering for specific people in your size breakdown. We will help you figure out ways to ensure everyone is happy.

Print Dancing
Due to fabic's elastic property, there can be up to a 1/4 inch print variance so the design position may vary slightly per print. If you discover designs that are way off please let us know and we'll work to resolve it.

T-Shirt Dyelots
It's a rare, but possible situation when your shirt colors are not exactly the same. (Example - browns are a little different shades.) That's due to two different dye lots. It's rare, but happens at times.

Mid-project Changes
Any changes to an approved t-shirt project could affect delivery times and add additional costs. You will be responsible to cover any mid-project changes.


Shipping & Deadlines


AMB3R cannot guarantee in hand dates for ground shipping. While we can assure you that we are good at predicting when we will not be able to provide AMB3R does not guarantee in Hand Dates. It is recommended that you allow 3-5 days of margin for providing "in Hand Dates".
The following issues are out of AMB3R's control;

      Product Shortages - In the event a customer requests a specific style number that is out of stock.
      Re-Samples Times - In the event a design must be resampled per the customer's request.
      Weather Delays - Issues with the weather cannot be incorporated with meeting in hand dates.
      Customer is responsible for all shipping costs
